Sushi (寿司)

Prep time: 40 min

Cook time: 20 min

Total time: 1 hr 25 min

Ingredients with an * are optional

Ingredients

  • 1/2 lb salmon (sushi grade)
  • 1/2 lb tuna (sushi grade)
  • Soy sauce
  • 2 cups Japanese short grain rice
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/4 cup rice vinegar
  • Wasabi of your choice

Steps

  1. Cook the rice
  2. Add the hot rice in large bowl
  3. Add rice vinegar, sugar, and salt
  4. Mix and let sit until cool
  5. Cut the fish against the grain
  6. Cut 3" long, 1" wide, and 1/4" thick
  7. Keep your hands wet with water
  8. Form mounds of rice
  9. Cup your hand and press with pointer and middle finger
  10. Press to compact the mound
  11. Once rice is formed place a dab of wasabi on rice
  12. Place fish on the mound and press gently
  13. Enjoy!
