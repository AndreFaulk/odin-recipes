Taco Rice (タコリスト)

Time required: 30min

Ingredients with an * are optional

Ingredients

  • White Rice
  • Ground Beef or Pork mix
  • Taco Seasoning
  • * 1 Tomato
  • * Black Olives
  • * 1 Avocado
  • * Corn Chips
  • * 1 Onion
  • * 1/2 Head of Letuce

Steps

  1. Cook the rice
  2. Dice tomato to preferred size
  3. Dice avocado to preferred size
  4. Dice or use cut olives
  5. Dice onion to preferred size
  6. Dice lettuce or cut into strips
  7. Crush corn chips to 1 cm size bits
  8. Brown ground beef/pork till no more pink is present
  9. Drain some of the oil
  10. Pour the seasoning and add the required amount of water
  11. Bring to a boil then simmer
  12. Scoop desired amount of rice into a bowl
  13. Place ground beef and desired ingredients
