Takoyaki (たこ焼き)

Prep time: 30 min

Cook time: 20 min

Note: Required item Takoyaki pan

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Flour
  • Soy Sauce
  • Ocopus/tako
  • Green onions
  • Tenkasu
  • Takoyaki Sauce
  • Kewpie mayo
  • Bonito flakes
  • Seaweed

Steps

  1. Mix batter with a whisk till no clumps left
  2. Prep all of the fillings
  3. Pour and fill oil in the pan
  4. Pour in batter into the compartments to the top
  5. Add filings and cook till the edges crisp up
  6. Flip 90 degrees and add more batter
  7. Repeat this till you have a ball
  8. Cook till crispy and brown
  9. Pop them off the mold and onto a plate
  10. Squeeze on some sauce and mayo
  11. Enjoy!
