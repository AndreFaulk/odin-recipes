Takoyaki (たこ焼き)

Time required: 30min

Ingredients with an * are optional

Ingredients

  • White Rice
  • Ground Beef or Pork mix
  • Taco Seasoning
  • * 1 Tomato
  • * Black Olives
  • * 1 Avocado
  • * Corn Chips
  • * 1 Onion

Steps

  1. Cook the rice
  2. Dice tomato to preferrd size
  3. Dice avocado to preferrd size
  4. Dice or use cut olives
  5. Dice onion to preferrd size
  6. Crush corn chips to 1 cm size bits
  7. Brown ground beef/pork till no more pink is present
  8. Drain some of the oil
  9. Pour the seasoning and add the required ammount of water
  10. Bring to a boil then simmer
  11. Scoop desired ammount of rice into a bowl
  12. Place ground beef and desired ingredients
